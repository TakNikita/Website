The Product

XR Controllers: Enhancing Virtual and Augmented Reality Interaction.

It is a hand held accessory, which coupled with a VR Head mounted device,allows users to interact with and navigate the virtual or augmented environment through buttons, sensors, haptic feedback, and sometimes gesture recognition. The controllers are wireless, ergonomic, and tailored to specific XR platforms, contributing to a more immersive and natural user experience.
