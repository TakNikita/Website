Altie Reality

Interactive Corporate Trainings With Immersive Technology

The gaming industry has kept pace with the latest technological trends, leveraging new technology to provide the most engaging experience to the gamers, While both AR and VR are popular in gaming, MR offers the best of both worlds (physical and digital), allowing for new interactions through voice-based and gesture inputs. 

The demand for immersive gaming has fuelled the adoption of MR technologies. The ability to be transported into the game, fighting aliens and demons, and exploring environments beyond imagination provide a great pull for gaming industry. MR games serve as a gateway into the metaverse and can amalgamate into a experience rich with immersive engagement, social interactions and e-commerce, making gaming a forerunner in potential and immediate opportunities from MR

The gamification is not limited to entertainment, but can also be leveraged to enhance engagement to industrial trainings by converting them into games, something that class room training has always been struggling with

 

MR use cases

  • Completely immersive experience with spatial sounds and gestures

  • Experience the game rather than just playing

  • Opportunities to expand into Metaverse and encompass social and commercial interactions

  • Improve user engagement for industrial tasks by gamification

  • Improve skills at sports by using AI based challenges

Benefits of MR in Gaming

Realistic experience and enhanced engagement by upto 2X

Improved physical movement and activity by over 500%

Over 5X more effective in overcoming phobias of height, fire, dark etc as compared to standard treatments

Improves hand eye coordination and spatial abilities by up to 50%

VR games are known to be up to 25% more effective with pain and PTSD over conventional therapies

Industrial Gamification can improve trainee engagement by over 250%

2X better reaction times in VR gamers as compared to regular gamers
